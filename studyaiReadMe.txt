# Study AI

Study AI is an AI-powered study assistant that helps students learn more efficiently by transforming study materials into interactive learning resources. Users can upload PDFs, notes, or YouTube lectures and automatically generate quizzes, flashcards, and summaries for better exam preparation.

---

## Features

- Upload **PDFs, notes, and YouTube lecture links**
- Automatic generation of **quizzes, flashcards, and summaries**
- Helps students **revise faster and prepare for exams**
- Interactive and structured **AI-assisted learning experience**
- Designed to reduce the need for **multiple study tools**

---

- Homepage

## Future Improvements

- Develop a **mobile application version** of Study AI for easier accessibility.
- Integrate an **AI chatbot tutor** to help students understand concepts instantly.
- Build a **teacher partnership system** where qualified teachers can earn side income by answering student questions.
- Implement a **question credibility system** to assign students to teachers with matching expertise levels.
- Allow students to **schedule appointments with teachers** for deeper learning support.

---

## Project Goal

The goal of Study AI is to create an **all-in-one intelligent study platform** that improves learning efficiency, simplifies exam preparation, and provides personalized academic support for students.
